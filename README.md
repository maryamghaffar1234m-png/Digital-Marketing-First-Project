## 🌍 Project Overview

This project is based on **Sustainable Development Goal 4 (Quality Education)**.
The main focus is to highlight the importance of **girls’ education in Pakistan** and raise awareness about the challenges they face.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Promote awareness about girls’ education
* Highlight gender disparity in literacy rate
* Encourage use of digital platforms for education
* Motivate people to support equal education opportunities

---

## 🎨 Part 1: Poster Design

The poster represents hope, empowerment, and the importance of education.

### 🔑 Key Elements:

* 📢 **Headline:** *"Unlock Her Future: Educate a Girl, Empower a Nation."*
* 📊 **Statistics:**

  * 12 Million girls out of school in Pakistan
  * 46% female literacy vs 69% male literacy
* 💡 **Message:** Education is every girl’s right

---
